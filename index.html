Professional Profile

I am a systematic, hands-on Network Engineer with a strong track record of engineering, deploying, and validating complex enterprise-level network and compute architectures. My core competencies lie in implementing and validating robust Layer 1-4 topologies, managing advanced IP addressing schemes, and maintaining deeply integrated environments utilizing dynamic routing protocols including OSPF, EIGRP, and RIP.

Driven by empirical data and network resilience, I excel in advanced performance testing, execution of comprehensive validation plans, and high-load traffic analysis using industry-leading evaluation platforms like Ixia and Spirent. I specialize in cross-layer troubleshooting and packet-level data capture utilizing Wireshark to reconstruct anomalies and perform precise Root Cause Analysis (RCA) within complex lab environments.

Additionally, I hold significant technical capabilities across network security hardenings and infrastructure virtualization. This includes configuring, provisioning, and testing Cisco Next-Generation security blueprints (ASA, Firepower/FTD) through FMC and FDM platforms, validating operational mechanisms such as NAT, ACLs, and IPsec VPNs. My system experience extends to virtualization stack administration on VMware ESXi platforms, alongside bare-metal hardware orchestration and server provisioning within Cisco UCS (CIMC) environments.
